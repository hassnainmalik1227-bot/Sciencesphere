Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about Science Sphere? We’ve answered some of the most common ones below. If you can’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to reach out through our Contact Page.

1. What is Science Sphere?

Science Sphere is an educational platform dedicated to simplifying complex scientific concepts. Our goal is to make science engaging and accessible to readers of all backgrounds.

2. Who writes the articles?

Our content is written by science enthusiasts, educators, and researchers with expertise across various fields—physics, biology, chemistry, and environmental science.

3. Can I share your articles on my website or social media?

Yes, you can share our content by linking directly to the original article on Science Sphere. However, copying or reproducing full articles without permission is not allowed.

4. How often are new blogs published?

We aim to publish new content weekly, covering trending scientific news, deep-dives into theories, and experiments you can try at home.

5. How do I submit a guest post?

We welcome guest submissions! If you’d like to contribute, please contact us via contact@sciencesphere.com with your topic idea and a brief author bio.

6. Do you fact-check your articles?

Absolutely. Each article undergoes research verification and editorial review to ensure accuracy and reliability.

7. Is Science Sphere affiliated with any organization?

No, we are an independent science communication project, not funded or sponsored by any corporate or academic entity.

8. Do you collect personal information?

We collect minimal data, primarily through analytics and voluntary contact forms. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

9. What should I do if I find an error in an article?

We appreciate feedback! Please send corrections or clarifications to contact@sciencesphere.com and we’ll review them promptly.

10. How can I support Science Sphere?

The best way to support us is by reading, sharing, and engaging with our content. You can also suggest topics or offer collaborations through our Contact Page.
